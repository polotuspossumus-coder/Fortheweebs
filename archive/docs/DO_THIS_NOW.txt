STEP 3: STRIPE CONNECT TO CHIME
========================================

1. Go to: https://dashboard.stripe.com/settings/connect
2. Click "Get started" or "Settings"
3. Under "Payout bank account":
   - Click "Add bank account"
   - Select "United States"
   - Enter your Chime routing & account numbers:
     * Routing number: Find in Chime app → Settings → Account Info
     * Account number: Find in Chime app → Settings → Account Info
   - Click "Add account"
4. Stripe will send 2 micro-deposits (takes 1-2 days)
5. Check Chime app for the amounts
6. Go back to Stripe dashboard
7. Click "Verify" and enter the 2 amounts
8. Done! Payouts will go to your Chime account

PAYOUT SCHEDULE:
- Default: Every 2 days
- Can change to: Daily, Weekly, or Monthly
- Go to: Stripe Dashboard → Settings → Bank accounts and scheduling
