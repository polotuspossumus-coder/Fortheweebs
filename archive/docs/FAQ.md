- **Streamers** - Real-time CGI effects for streams
- **Manga Artists** - Panel creation, Japanese text support
- **Content Creators** - Anyone making anime-related content

### Do I need to download software?
**Nope!** ForTheWeebs runs entirely in your web browser. Just sign up and start creating. We also have mobile apps for iOS and Android.

### Is there a free version?
**Yes!** Our free tier includes:

We have **AI-powered copyright detection** that:

- Protects you and the platform

### Can I upload copyrighted content?
**No.** Our AI moderation system blocks:

**You CAN upload**:
- Your own original artwork
- Content you have permission to use
- Transformative/fair use content (review, commentary, parody)

### Is adult content allowed?
**Yes, with restrictions:**
- Must have Adult ($20/mo) tier or higher
- Must pass age verification (18+)
- Cannot be illegal content (CSAM, etc.)
- Must follow our content policy

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting

### The website is slow or laggy
Try these fixes:
1. Close other browser tabs
2. Disable browser extensions
3. Clear browser cache
4. Check your internet speed
5. Try a different browser
6. Use the desktop/mobile app instead

### My export failed
Common causes:
1. **File too large** - Check your storage limit
2. **Internet disconnected** - Make sure you're online
3. **Browser closed** - Keep tab open during export
4. **Timeout** - Large files may take time, be patient

### I can't upload files
Check:
1. **File size** - Free tier: 100MB limit
2. **Storage limit** - You may be out of space
3. **File type** - We support: JPG, PNG, GIF, MP4, WebM
4. **Copyright** - File may be blocked for copyright

### Features are locked even though I paid
1. Make sure payment went through (check email receipt)
2. Log out and log back in
3. Check your subscription status in settings
4. Contact support if still locked

### I forgot my password
1. Go to login page
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your email
4. Check email for reset link
5. Create new password
